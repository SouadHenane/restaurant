About Us

DELICIOUS FOOD...

KingFood’s is known to have the best Food in Sidi Bel Abbès and on the side of the west region.

Recognized since the nineties as Sidi Bel Abbès City’s best brunch spot, KingFood’s has grown to be a Sidi Bel Abbès City Upper East Side Landmark that is visited by both locals and tourists from all over the world.The Chefs have re-created recipes that were the staples of” Home Cooked Meals”.

Even more amazing is that the Blue Plate Specials menu changes everyday. KingFood’s features Lunch and Dinner menus that feature comfort food favorites for everyone. Locals and tourists thrive on dining in an authentic Sidi Bel Abbès City diner, which is why KingFood’s has become a Sidi Bel Abbès City "must visit" landmark destination.
